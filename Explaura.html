ABOUT ME

Forget about Dora the Explorer. I'm Laura the Explaura.

My name is Laura Jane. Common aliases include Laura, LJ, LoJane, BooBoos, One-K, and Paula. I am a daughter, a sister, a dog lady, a nail artist, a traveler, a student, a novice informaticist, a professional bridesmaid, an aspiring hand letterer, a Hufflepuff, a terrible but passionate karaoke performer, a nurse, a self-learned plumber, an expert on all things Taylor Swift, and a lover of eyeglasses. I call Texas, California, Thailand, England, Montana, and New York my homes.

Join me as I delve into new hobbies and foray into unchartered territories. Whether its traveling to a new city to breathe in the culture or just sitting on my apartment floor to fine-tune a skill, I'm ready for the challenge. I'm here to EXPLORE anything and everything that sets a spark in my heart and in my mind.
